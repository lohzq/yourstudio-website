For dogs, we require up-to-date core vaccinations (DHPP and Rabies) plus Bordetella (kennel cough). For cats, we require FVRCP and Rabies vaccinations. All pets must also be on regular flea and tick prevention. Please bring your pet's vaccination records on the first visit.
Yes! We offer pet pickup and delivery within the East Coast area (Bedok, Tampines, Marine Parade, Katong, Siglap). Rates start from $15 one-way depending on distance. Just let us know when booking and we'll arrange everything. We use secure, comfortable pet carriers for transport.
