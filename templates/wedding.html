Our Story

A studio born from a love of beautiful details

Founded in 2018, Bloom & Co. has designed over 200 weddings across Singapore's most iconic venues. We believe every couple deserves a celebration that reflects their unique love story.

Our team of six planners and florists brings together decades of experience in luxury events, fine dining, and floral artistry. We're perfectionists, romantics, and — above all — listeners.
