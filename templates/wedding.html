What We Do

Curated Services

Every detail, thoughtfully designed. Every moment, beautifully orchestrated. We bring your vision to life with grace and intention.

Wedding Planning

From concept to celebration, we manage every aspect of your wedding with meticulous care — venue sourcing, vendor coordination, timeline creation, and budget management, so you can savour every moment.

Floral Design

Our signature floral installations transform spaces into living works of art. From romantic bridal bouquets to breathtaking ceremony arches and cascading table centrepieces, each bloom is chosen with intention.

Styling & Decor

Our Story

Crafting timeless
celebrations since 2016

Bloom & Co. was born from a simple belief: that your wedding day should feel as natural and effortless as falling in love. Founded by creative director Isabelle Tan, our boutique studio brings together a curated team of planners, florists, and stylists who share a passion for understated elegance.

Based in the heart of Singapore, we've had the privilege of designing celebrations at the city's most prestigious venues — from the colonial grandeur of Raffles Hotel to the tropical intimacy of Capella Sentosa. Every wedding we touch is a reflection of the couple's unique journey, told through thoughtful details, exquisite florals, and seamless orchestration.

We don't believe in cookie-cutter weddings. We believe in yours.

Isabelle Tan
Founder & Creative Director
