Our Story

A tradition of excellence, reimagined

Nestled in the heart of the city, Maison is where time-honoured French culinary traditions meet bold contemporary vision. Every dish is an act of devotion — sourced from the finest artisanal producers, composed with precision, and presented with a reverence for the craft that has defined great dining for generations.

Our intimate 40-seat dining room invites you into an atmosphere of quiet luxury, where candlelight dances across aged oak and every detail has been considered. This is not merely a meal. It is a memory.

Chef Laurent Dubois

Executive Chef & Founder
