Our Story

Made with intention.

Little & Co. began with two parents who wanted better for their baby. We couldn't find essentials that were truly safe, sustainably sourced, and beautifully designed — so we made our own.

Every product is crafted from certified organic materials, produced in ethical facilities, and designed to grow alongside your family. We're proudly family-owned and committed to leaving a gentler footprint.
